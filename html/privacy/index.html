Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 18, 2025

Overview

PrimeState is a personal wellness tracking app designed with privacy as a core principle.

Your data stays on your device.

Data Collection

What we collect: Nothing. PrimeState does not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers.

What stays on your device:

  • State check-ins (focus, energy, clarity scores)
  • Consumption logs (food, drinks, supplements)
  • Activity logs (exercise, movement)
  • Patterns and insights generated from your data
  • App preferences and settings

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using secure on-device storage. We do not have access to your data, and it never leaves your device.

No Account Required

PrimeState does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app.

Third-Party Services

PrimeState does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertisers.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time through the app's Settings. Uninstalling the app will also remove all stored data.
