Common questions

What builders ask.

Do I give up equity in my company?
No. This is revenue sharing, not equity. You keep 100% ownership of your company. The creator earns a percentage of revenue for as long as the partnership is active. Either side can exit with 30 days notice.
What if the creator doesn't actually promote my product?
Every partnership has minimum promotion requirements. If a creator's activity drops below the threshold, their revenue share pauses automatically. The AI also monitors promotion quality and flags underperformance early.
How is the revenue split determined?
Our AI evaluates both sides — your product's metrics, the creator's audience quality, predicted conversion rates, and the marginal revenue the creator is expected to drive. It outputs a specific, justified percentage. No negotiation required.
What does my product need to qualify?
A working product with real users. We verify through Stripe Connect — if you're processing payments, you qualify. Pre-revenue products with a free user base may qualify with a higher creator split to offset risk.
Can I end a partnership?
Yes. Either side can terminate with 30 days notice. No exit fees, no penalty. Clean break. Revenue share stops when the notice period ends.
How do I know the creator's audience is real?
Creators connect their social accounts via OAuth. Our AI analyzes engagement patterns, audience authenticity, bot ratios, and comment quality. We set a minimum engagement floor and filter out inflated accounts before you ever see them.
