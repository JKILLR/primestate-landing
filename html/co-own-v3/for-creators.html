Common questions

What creators ask.

How do I know the revenue numbers are real?
All product revenue flows through Stripe Connect. The platform has full financial visibility. Builders can't hide or inflate numbers — what you see in your dashboard is what Stripe processes.
What's the minimum audience size?
We focus on quality over quantity. A creator with 10K highly engaged followers in a specific niche is more valuable than 500K passive followers. Our AI evaluates engagement quality, not just follower count.
How much do I need to promote?
Each partnership has minimum activity requirements — typically 3-4 authentic posts per month featuring the product. If activity drops below the threshold, the revenue share pauses until you resume. It's designed to be sustainable, not a content treadmill.
How is my revenue split determined?
Our AI evaluates your audience quality, engagement rates, niche relevance, and predicted conversion potential — alongside the product's metrics. It outputs a specific split percentage based on the value each side brings. Typical creator splits range from 18-35%.
Can I promote competing products?
You can partner with multiple products, but not direct competitors in the same category during an active partnership. If you co-own a productivity app, you wouldn't simultaneously promote a rival. Different categories are fine.
What if a product I co-own shuts down?
Our AI monitors product health — uptime, MRR trends, user activity. If metrics decline significantly, you're notified early so you can adjust your portfolio. If a product shuts down, the partnership ends and you keep everything you've earned.
